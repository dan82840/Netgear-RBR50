Your Satellite is not connecting to the Orbi network

This is most likely due to one of the following reasons.

1. It might be too far away from Orbi Network

Please turn the Satellite off, move it closer, and back on. Wait 2 minutes for Satellite to complete start up.

2. Your Satellite might not setup successfully

Make sure both your Base and Satellites are turned on, press WPS button on both devices. Wait for 2 minutes to complete setup.
